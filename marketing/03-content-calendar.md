# 03 — 14-Day Social Content Calendar

> Pillar 2 · Social Media Strategy · 3 marks
> Channels: **Facebook Page + Instagram Business** (cross-posted via Meta Business Suite where format allows).

## Posting cadence rules

- **Frequency:** 1 post/day on each platform (14 posts × 2 = 28 total).
- **Best times** (Pakistan Standard Time, validated against Meta Business Suite *Insights → Best times*):
  - Weekdays: **10:30 AM** (commute) and **8:30 PM** (evening scroll)
  - Weekends: **11:00 AM**
- **Format mix per week:** 3 Reels · 2 Carousels · 1 Single-image · 1 Story-only day. (Meets KPI #6 — *3+ formats used.*)
- **Hashtag strategy:** 8–12 hashtags per IG post, blend of:
  - 2 branded (`#VidIQ #AIVideoIntelligence`)
  - 4 broad (`#AI #ProductivityTools #StudyHack #ContentCreators`)
  - 4–6 niche (`#YouTubeSummary #AIForStudents #LectureNotes #VideoToText #LiveStreamAI`)

## Content pillars (rotation)

| Pillar | Share | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Show the product** (P) | 35 % | Screen-recordings, before/after, feature spotlights. |
| **Use cases** (U) | 25 % | Student, educator, creator, trader scenarios. |
| **Educate** (E) | 20 % | "How AI summarises a video", multimodal AI 101. |
| **Social proof / story** (S) | 10 % | User quotes, build-in-public moments. |
| **Lighten up** (L) | 10 % | Memes, polls, "vs." comparisons, behind-the-scenes. |
