# 02 — 30-Second Video Ad

> Pillar 1 · Branding · 3 marks · *"Sizzle + value proposition"*

## 1. Strategic brief

| | |
|---|---|
| **Goal** | Drive sign-ups (`/analyze`) — emotional hook + clear value prop. |
| **Audience** | Students cramming long lectures, content creators researching topics, professionals staring at 90-min webinars. |
| **Single core promise** | *"Watch the highlight reel of every video before you commit your hour."* |
| **Tone** | Confident, calm, slightly cinematic — never frantic. |
| **Format** | 30 s · 9:16 vertical (Reels / Shorts / TikTok) and 1:1 square (FB feed cutdown). |
| **Music** | Synth-driven, building (cinematic-tech, 95 BPM). Royalty-free options: *"Resolve" — Pictures of the Floating World* (CC-BY) or Epidemic Sound *"Subliminal"*. |
| **End frame** | VidIQ wordmark + *"vidiq.app — analyse any video"*. |

## 2. The 30-second script — *"The Long Bar"*

| Time | Visual | VO / Caption | Audio |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0:00 – 0:03** | Close-up: phone screen with a YouTube progress bar reading **`0:42 / 1:48:00`** — the hand barely lets go. | **VO:** *"You hit play on a 90-minute video…"* | Faint UI tap. Music intro. |
| **0:03 – 0:06** | Smash cut. Same screen, time-lapse: bar inches forward as the room dims, day turns to night. | **VO:** *"…and lose an evening."* | Music drops half a beat. |
| **0:06 – 0:10** | Hard cut to **VidIQ dashboard** (live screen-record). YouTube URL pasted into the Analyse form. *Click.* | **Caption (lower third):** *"What if you could read it instead?"* | Soft *whoosh*. |
| **0:10 – 0:18** | Quick montage, beat-matched to music: <br>• transcript scrolling <br>• keyframes flashing in <br>• summary tabs filling <br>• chat panel typing *"Where does she explain attention heads?"* with a timestamp citation appearing. | **Caption beats:** <br>*"Transcript."* <br>*"Keyframes."* <br>*"Summary."* <br>*"Chat — with citations."* | Musical build. UI tick on each caption. |
| **0:18 – 0:23** | Pull back: VidIQ workspace fully populated. Cursor clicks a chapter — video player jumps to that exact moment. | **VO:** *"Every claim is grounded in the source."* | Click + seek SFX. |
| **0:23 – 0:27** | Cut to user's face — relaxed, half-smiling, closing the laptop. Phone shows a green check: **"Ready in 47 s."** | **VO:** *"Your hour back."* | Music resolves. |
| **0:27 – 0:30** | Black. VidIQ logo (white-bg variant) zooms in from infinity, settles. Tagline below: ***AI Video Intelligence***. URL beneath: *vidiq.app*. | **VO:** *"VidIQ. Watch less. Learn more."* | Final swell + sting. |

## 4. Voice-over notes

- **Reader:** Female, mid-20s to mid-30s, calm UK or neutral-American. Avoid
  hyped American "ad-voice."
- **Direction:** Speak as if you're tipping a friend off — not selling.
- **Total VO copy:** 38 words → ~12 s of speech, leaves 18 s of music + caption beats.

## 5. Captions (burned-in — required for FB/IG silent autoplay)

```
You hit play on a 90-minute video…
…and lose an evening.
What if you could read it instead?
Transcript. Keyframes. Summary. Chat — with citations.
Every claim is grounded in the source.
Your hour back.
VidIQ — watch less, learn more.
```

## 7. Production checklist (for *you* to execute — see `13-self-do-checklist.md`)

- [ ] Record screen-capture of VidIQ in dark mode at **1080×1920** (vertical) and **1920×1080** (wide). Use OBS or QuickTime.
- [ ] Shoot one **B-roll close-up** of phone with a real YouTube long video on it (use a dimming room — single key light + colour gel).
- [ ] Shoot one **face shot** of the user closing the laptop (3–5 s).
- [ ] License music (Epidemic Sound free trial or YouTube Audio Library).
- [ ] Edit in CapCut / Premiere / DaVinci Resolve. Use the brand violet
      `#a855f7` as the only colour grade tint on captions and end-card.
- [ ] Export 30 s, 15 s, 6 s, 10 s versions per the table above.
- [ ] Save raw + master files to `marketing/assets/video/` (folder you create
      locally — too heavy for git).
