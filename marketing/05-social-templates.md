# 05 — Social Templates: Welcome, Auto-Replies, Caption Library

> Pillar 2 · Social Media Strategy · supports KPI #9 — *Automated message / Welcome note*

## 1. Page welcome message (Messenger / Instagram DM)

Configure under **Meta Business Suite → Inbox → Automations → Greeting message**.

> 👋 Hi, **{{first_name}}** — welcome to VidIQ.
>
> We turn any YouTube video or live stream into a transcript, timestamped
> summary, and a chat you can ground every answer in.
>
> Reply with the link of any video and we'll show you. Or try it now →
> **vidiq.app/analyze**
>
> *(A real human picks up here Mon–Fri, 10 AM – 6 PM PKT.)*

## 2. Instant reply (page closed / outside hours)

Configure under **Inbox → Automations → Away message**.

> Thanks, **{{first_name}}** 🌙 — we're away right now and reply within
> **24 hours**.
>
> Don't wait for us — paste any video URL into **vidiq.app/analyze** and
